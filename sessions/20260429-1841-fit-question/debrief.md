## Mission is the Boss

### Strengths

- The mission is genuine and relatable: make open source accessible to juniors
- You built from scratch with real ownership — recruiting, roles, tech decisions
- The tech stack pivot shows real conviction: scrapping everything mid-project takes guts

### Weaknesses

- You never led with *why the mission mattered to you personally*. You jumped straight to what you built. Mission is the Boss is about personal motivation and long-term thinking — start there.
